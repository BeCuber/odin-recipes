Wild Berrys

Description:

Let's make a sample and delicious sandwich by adding some of your favourite ingredients between two slices of bread

Ingredients:

  • Two slices of bread
  • 1 or 2 lettuce leaf
  • 2 slice of ham
  • 2 slices of cheese

Steps:

  1. Put one of the slices of bread on a dish and reserve the other one
  2. Add over that slice the rest of ingredients. Order doesn't matter.
  3. Finish it by adding the second slice of bread above.
