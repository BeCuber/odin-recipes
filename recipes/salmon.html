Savage Salmon


Description:

To recieve the divine blessings you deserve, this recipe has to be made in the traditional way, just as the John West comercial shows.


Ingredients:

  • Courage
  • Basic hand-to-hand combat skills
  • A small amount of parsley

Steps:

  1. Look for a good spot for salmon fishing (try to maximize your time by picking up berrys along the way). The more bears you encounter, the better the fishing spot.
  2. Wait until a bear catches a salmon. Consider that if you try to catch a salmon yourself, there might be other bears waiting to steal it from you instead.
  3. Use your combat skills to temporarily knock out the bear, and steal the salmon.
  4. Run for your life.
  5. When your sacred mead is ready, pour yourself a jar and build a campfire.
  6. Roast the salmon over the campfire and serve it with a sprinkle of parsley.
