Sacred Mead


Description:

It is said that mead is the only food Odin consumes, but continue preparing the rest of the recipes. Even he knows that mead is enjoyed more with something to snack on.

The traditional way to obtain honey involves activities that may lead to a confilct between you and bees... and, perhaps, even encounter a bear.

You might think this is an opportunity to prove yourself, but nowadays there are fewer bees, and Odin supports efforts that don't harm bees while ensuring a steady supply of mead.

Therefore, there is no shame in buying ingredients for this recipe or tricking the bees by approaching their honeycomb in a bee-disguise. The choice is yours.


Ingredients:

  • Honey
  • Water
  • Yeast

Steps:

  1. Warm the water.
  2. Add the honey and stir.
  3. Remove any wax and other foreign bodies from the top of the mixture and add more water if needed.
  4. Once you are satisfied with the colour and texture, add the yeast and transfer the mixture to a barrel.
  5. Allow the mead to ferment for approximately a month and a half. You can use this time to prepare the other recipes.
